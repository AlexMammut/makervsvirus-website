Using our Maker skills against Corona

This website is intended to bring together people and organisations who need currently hard-to-find protective equipment or spare parts with makers and makerspaces who can possibly produce them. Behind this project is a small German and English speaking organization team.

Please note: We only list initiatives that offer their (self) produced items for free or at cost price.

For people and organisations who want to support this project

We're looking for people who:

  • make 3D prints
  • have a laser cutter available
  • want to help with logistics
  • want to help with the organization

The goal is for people to come together in a self-organized way to form (local or international) production hubs, which we can list on this website. For this purpose we offer the following Slack workspace to find fellow Makers or existing hubs in need of help. If you want to register as a new producing hub, please write in the Slack channel called "Register Hub".
